Columbia LABS · TeaChart · Mar 2026

TeaChart analysis portal

TeaChart extends the LABS site with a portal for tea intake versus BAG, proteomics, metabolomics, IDPs, and related outputs. Modeling mirrors the team’s Sleep Chart paper-style settings (e.g., GAM summaries). The PI needed pages browseable early and easy to refresh as tables landed during peer review.

GWAS subpages were iterated by the PI first; then shared data drops (internal paths not listed here) fed section updates. Home highlights significant organs and uses a purple system background aligned with sibling portals such as MUTATE.

Live site: https://labs-laboratory.com/teachart/
